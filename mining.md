# Mining

**FLO Mining at a Glance:'**

* Proof of Work \(PoW\) - sCrypt algorithm
* Block Time: 40 seconds
* Total Coin Supply:  160,000,000
* Halving: Every 800,000 blocks
* Next Halving: Block 4,000,000 \(~February 21, 2020\)
